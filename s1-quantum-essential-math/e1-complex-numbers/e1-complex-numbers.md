# S1E1 - Complex Numbers Cheatsheet

## Quadratic equation
$$
αx^2+βx+γ=0
$$

### (i) $Δ=0$
$$
x = \frac{-β}{2α}
$$

### (ii) $Δ>0$
$$
x=\frac{-β\pm\sqrt{{β}^2-4{αγ}}}{2α}
$$

### (iii) $Δ<0$
$$
x =\frac{-β\pmi\sqrt{|{β}^2-4{αγ}|}}{2α}
$$

## Euler's formula
$$
e^{iθ}=cos(θ)+isin(θ)
$$

## Euler's identity
$$
e^{iθ}+1=0
$$

## Complex numbers and polar representation
$$
z=α+iβ=r(cos(θ)+isin(θ))=re^{iθ}
$$
