# s1.e1 - Discover Complex Numbers

## Quadratic equation
$$ \begin{equation} 
αx^2+βx+γ=0 , \quad Δ=β^2-4αγ
\end{equation} $$

### i) $Δ=0$:
$$ \begin{equation}
x = \frac{-β}{2α}
\end{equation} $$

### ii) $Δ>0$:
$$ \begin{equation}
x=\frac{-β\pm\sqrt{Δ}}{2α}
\end{equation} $$

### iii) $Δ<0$:
$$ \begin{equation}
x =\frac{-β\pm i\sqrt{|Δ|}}{2α}
\end{equation} $$

$$ \begin{equation}
Re(x)=\frac{-β}{2α}
\end{equation} $$

$$ \begin{equation}
Im(x)=\frac{\sqrt{|Δ|}}{2α}
\end{equation} $$

## Imaginary numbers
$$ \begin{equation}
i=\sqrt{-1}
\end{equation} $$

$$ \begin{equation}
i^2=-1
\end{equation} $$
